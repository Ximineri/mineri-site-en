MINĒRI is not a place, it is a signature.

This proposal formalizes the transformation of MINĒRI into a personal brand embodied by its creator, Xi Minēri. The project crystallizes around a unique location: Villa Minēri, a private residence and creative workshop in the heart of Hoi An. It's an invitation to step into a creator's world, to share his art of living, and to witness the act of creation firsthand.

A Partnership Magnifying Your Asset & Your Revenue

We are not looking for a landlord; we are looking for a partner. We are committed to maintaining your villa in pristine condition, enhancing its value and prestige. Our model directly involves you in our total success: you receive a fixed rent, plus a 15% commission on the net profits from all MINĒRI activities, including perfume sales, workshops, and private events. This offers a potential income far superior to a classic rental agreement.

The Alchemical Atelier: The Heart of the Brand

The beating heart of Villa Minēri is the Alchemical Atelier, the cabinet of curiosities where Xi Minēri, a perfumer and oenologist, crafts unique "olfactory vintages."

The Art of Sublimation

We do not sell nights or products; we offer access to a universe. Each service is a facet of Xi Minēri's art of living, from the discovery of the atelier to tasting masterclasses and cultural events.
