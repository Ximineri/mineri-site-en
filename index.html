MINĒRI is not a place, it is a signature.

This proposal formalizes the transformation of MINĒRI into a personal brand embodied by its creator, Xi Minēri. The project crystallizes around a unique location: Villa Minēri, a private residence and creative workshop in the heart of Hoi An. It's an invitation to step into a creator's world, to share his art of living, and to witness the act of creation firsthand. [cite: 88, 89, 97]

A Partnership Magnifying Your Asset & Your Revenue

We are not looking for a landlord; we are looking for a partner. We are committed to maintaining your villa in pristine condition, enhancing its value and prestige through our high standards. Our model directly involves you in our total success: you receive a fixed rent, plus a 15% commission on the net profits from all MINĒRI activities[cite: 95], including perfume sales, workshops, and private events. This offers a potential income far superior to a classic rental agreement. [cite: 96]

The Universe of the Creator

The beating heart of Villa Minēri is the Perfume Atelier, the creative studio where Xi Minēri, an Oenologist & Perfumer, crafts unique "olfactory vintages."

The Art of Conviviality

Article 1: Purpose of the Agreement

This agreement aims at the provision by the Owner to the Master Lessee of the property described hereinafter, for its commercial exploitation under the MINĒRI brand, dedicated to luxury tourist accommodation and event organization. This partnership seeks to maximize the Villa's value and generate optimized revenues for both Parties.

Article 2: Property Description

This agreement pertains to the luxury villa located at [FULL ADDRESS OF THE VILLA], including [NUMBER] bedrooms and facilities such as a private swimming pool, garden, etc.

Article 3: Term of the Agreement and Renewal Conditions

The initial term of this Master Lease Agreement is for twelve (12) consecutive months. At the end of the initial term, the agreement shall be tacitly renewed for successive periods of twelve (12) months, unless either Party notifies the other of its intention not to renew at least three (3) months before the expiry of the current term.

Article 4: Financial Conditions

Monthly Fixed Rent: The Master Lessee undertakes to pay the Owner a fixed monthly rent of 3,000 USD (Three Thousand United States Dollars). This is a negotiable baseline.

Commission on Gross Revenue: In addition to the fixed rent, the Master Lessee undertakes to pay the Owner a commission of 15% (Fifteen percent) on the net profits generated by all MINĒRI activities.

Article 5: Property Use and Commercial Authorization

The Owner expressly authorizes the company Thang Long Event (operating under the MINĒRI brand) to use the Villa exclusively for commercial purposes, including luxury tourist accommodation and as an event venue. The Owner grants the Master Lessee the irrevocable right to sublease the Villa to third parties (Guests) under its full and sole responsibility.

Article 6: Allocation of Responsibilities and Villa Maintenance

Owner's Responsibilities: The Owner remains responsible for the structural elements and heavy infrastructure of the Villa.
MINĒRI's Responsibilities: MINĒRI undertakes the operational management and routine maintenance.

C. Specific Responsibility: Deep Cleaning & Laundry Management

Article 7: Insurance and Coverage Responsibilities

The Owner undertakes to take out and maintain a multi-risk home insurance policy. MINĒRI undertakes to take out and maintain a Professional Civil Liability / Operations Insurance.

Article 8: General Obligations of the Parties

The Owner shall make the Villa available in perfect working and habitable condition. The Master Lessee shall use the Villa in accordance with the defined purpose.

Article 9: Dispute Resolution

Any dispute shall be resolved by prioritizing amicable solutions through a phased approach: Negotiation, Mediation, Arbitration, and finally Judicial Recourse.

Article 10: Early Termination Conditions

The contract may be terminated by either party in the event of a serious and repeated breach of its contractual obligations by the other party.

Article 11: General Provisions

This agreement shall be governed by the laws of Vietnam. Any amendment must be made in writing.
